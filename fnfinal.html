BATTLE ROYALE: THE CORE

Zero Build vs. Building

Choose your fight. Master the art of rapid construction to gain the high ground and defend yourself in the classic mode, or jump into Zero Build for a pure, gun-on-gun survival experience. Both modes offer the same iconic map and chaotic fun, but require vastly different strategies.

  • Classic Mode: Resource gathering and strategic defense.
  • Zero Build: Focus on positioning and map awareness.
  • Daily and weekly quests for XP and rewards.

THE ISLAND: MYTHS & LOCATIONS

Tilted Towers

The most legendary drop spot. High risk, high reward. A true test of fast reflexes and close-quarters combat.

Loot Lake

The calm center of the map, often hiding secrets and serving as a key rotation point for the final circles.

Wailing Woods

Dense forests offering cover and resources, a favorite for players who prefer a stealthier approach to the match.

The Zero Point

The nexus of all reality in the Fortnite universe. It controls the loop and drives all major story events.
